In the midst of chaotic place out of nowhere, I met a Chinese-looking girl-a Chindo. Not really my typical Chindo's face I was familiar with but I like her personality anyway. Very cheerful and good-vibes kind of person. 

She initiated the first move and promptly got my attention.  
She had a pure heart by nature.

We escalated things. Now we are a good match trying to get to know one another.  
I figured it out, it was more than a lucky draw, I found a kind-hearted rare soul. Completely wholesome and compassionate.

An unexpectedly sweet dream.
I woke up with the feeling of excitement, even though it was just a dream, I can vividly remember it.
